🕹️ How to Play

  • 🚦 The game starts with a single color flashing.
  • 🧠 Remember the sequence of the flashing colors.
  • 🎯 Click the colors in the same order.
  • 🌟 With each round, a new color is added to the sequence, making it progressively harder.
  • ❌ The game ends when you click the wrong color or fail to replicate the sequence correctly.

Try to see how long you can keep up without making a mistake! GOOD LUCK 🎉
